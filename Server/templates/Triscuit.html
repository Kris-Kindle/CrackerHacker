Triscuits
  • 2 eggs
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 24 Triscuit Dill & Olive Oil Crackers
  • 1-1/2 cups seafood salad
  • 24 fresh dill sprigs
  1. Heat oven to 350°F.
  2. Whisk eggs and water in shallow dish until blended. Add 6 crackers; press to evenly cover. Let stand 2 to 3 min. or until softened. Press 1 soaked cracker onto bottom and up side of 6 mini muffin pan cups sprayed with cooking spray.
  3. Repeat with remaining crackers to make a total of 24 crusts.
  4. Bake 25 min. Cool.
  5. Fill cracker cups with salad just before serving. Top with dill.
